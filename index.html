Audits and Support

Once the initial stages of development are complete the focus then switches to feedback and customer review. Customer Feedback is integral to providing a catered development experience to our clients. Site changes typically come back in the form of support request and direct communication. Support Request originate in from our HubSpot portal and through Monday are assigned to the corresponding team. All changes are tracked in Monday.com and all corresponding discussion around these changes happens in Emails, Micrsoft Teams, Skype, Etc.

Native Development

At present this aspect of our developement team is primarily handled overseas, via Sumit and the Boys. However, going forward I believe that we can begin to lay the foundation for a greater contribution from our internal development team in regards to native theme and plugin development. And we can do it through, Docker!

Containerize the Cloudways setup in a Dockerfile with docker-compose.yaml. Creating a local development environement that is setup like our Cloudways environment, will allow the developement team to do more efficient and optimized native development on both themes and plugins

A complete dockerfile will have the necessary configuration to encapsulate an entire cloudways VPS setup, which includes: PHP, MySQL/MariaDB, Apache/Nginx (w/ modules), Git, Varnish, and Memcached
